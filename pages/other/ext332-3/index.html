24 Sealed Pistachios was presented in collaboration with guest curator by Tyler Cann. 24 sealed pistachios were arranged in the gallery and a handout informed visitors that if they could open the nut, it was theirs to take. Of the 24, 9 were opened. In addition to hands, visitors used a pair of pliers, a shoe, and a brick, to open the nuts. On the night of January 16th or the early morning of January 17th the remaining nuts and the opened shells were discarded by a member of the office cleaning crew, bringing the exhibition to a premature (but fitting) end.
